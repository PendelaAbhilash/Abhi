About AbHi Farms

AbHi Farms – Growing with CARE

At AbHi Farms, we are committed to delivering healthy, high-quality, and naturally raised farm products with care and dedication. Our farm specializes in poultry farming, including both broiler and country hens, along with livestock care and sustainable farming practices.

We believe farming is not just a business — it is a responsibility towards health, nature, and the community. With a focus on hygiene, animal welfare, and quality nutrition, AbHi Farms ensures that every product reflects trust, freshness, and care.

Our mission is to combine traditional farming values with modern practices to provide reliable farm products while supporting sustainable agriculture for future generations.
